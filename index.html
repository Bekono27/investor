The Opportunity
Strictly Confidential
A Market Hiding in Plain Sight
Vietnam has 55 million workers. Most are paid monthly. Most cannot access their earnings between paydays.
The Pain
Financial Vulnerability
When emergencies arise between paydays, Vietnamese workers resort to black market lending applications charging 20–30% per month in interest. This affects the majority of the workforce and represents an acute, systemic financial vulnerability.
The Market
Significant Opportunity
Vietnam's P2P lending market is projected to reach $4.1B USD by 2027. Earned Wage Access (EWA) is a $2.9B USD market across Southeast Asia, with Vietnam representing the fastest growing segment in the region.
The Gap
First Mover Position
No existing operator in Vietnam combines a live, revenue-generating P2P lending platform with an Earned Wage Access product on a single infrastructure. DigiLend is the first to do so and has already proven the model.
